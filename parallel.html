Mystery of Life

MYSTERY

The great mystery of life....
Life is too mysterious,
Too long to count on faults,
But quite short to love others,
Yet ideal to build great exalts...

Here every morning gives us opportunities,
Every second accelerates a new start,
Still tomorrows are the most awaited guests,
And we people call ourselves smart!

Every second we come closer too death,
Yet birthdays are gala celebrations,
Death allows us to meet the almighty,
Yet they are mourning occasions...

We are the creators of our own destiny,
Yet zodiacs are so very popular,
Lies are evident, yet ignored,
Truths are sins, search them with a binocular!

Relations are the pillars of our life,
Quite weak yet we live for so long,
It's so very difficult to state somebody right,
But easiest to describe somebody as wrong....

Humans thrive for faithful relationships,
Still possess the quality of betrayal,
Sky is the limit for imaginations,
But it's so very tough to know somebody's real portrayal..

The ones with limitless patience,
Are often described as fools,
The great geniuses with remarkable minds,
Have actually got no place in the schools...

Uniqueness is often criticized,
Following the majority is the ideal path,
Wrongs are appreciated or ignored,
But good deeds are the deservers of wrath...

You may think that life is easy,
Its strange but quite rife,
But mind my words friends,
It's really tough to reveal the great mystery of life....
